Natural Pest Control Methods

Using natural methods for pest control helps maintain soil health and protects the environment. Some effective strategies include:

  • Introducing natural predators like ladybugs and spiders.
  • Using neem oil and garlic spray to deter pests.
  • Crop rotation to prevent pest infestations.
  • Companion planting with marigolds and basil.

These eco-friendly techniques ensure a sustainable and productive farm.
