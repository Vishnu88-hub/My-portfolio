Soil Preparation for Organic Farming

Proper soil preparation is crucial for successful organic farming. Ensuring nutrient-rich, well-drained soil improves crop yield and sustainability.

Steps for Soil Preparation

  • Test the soil for pH and nutrient levels.
  • Add organic compost and natural fertilizers.
  • Use cover crops to enrich soil quality.
  • Practice crop rotation for soil health.
  • Ensure proper aeration and water retention.
