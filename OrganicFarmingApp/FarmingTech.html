Sustainable Farming Techniques

Explore various sustainable techniques for organic farming.

  • Crop Rotation - Helps maintain soil fertility and reduces pests.
  • Composting - Enhances soil health with organic waste recycling.
  • Water Conservation - Implements rainwater harvesting and drip irrigation.
  • Integrated Pest Management - Uses natural predators and biological control.
  • Agroforestry - Combines trees with crops for enhanced biodiversity.
  • Minimal Tillage - Reduces soil erosion and improves water retention.
  • Organic Mulching - Helps in moisture retention and weed control.
  • Use of Cover Crops - Prevents soil erosion and improves fertility.
