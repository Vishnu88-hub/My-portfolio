Step 3: Natural Pest Control

Protecting crops from pests is essential for organic farming. Instead of chemical pesticides, use natural methods such as:

  • Companion Planting: Grow plants that repel pests naturally, such as marigolds with vegetables.
  • Neem Oil: A natural pesticide that deters insects and protects crops.
  • Beneficial Insects: Introduce ladybugs and praying mantises to control aphids and harmful pests.
  • Crop Rotation: Prevents pest infestations by alternating plant families in different seasons.
  • Physical Barriers: Use nets, row covers, and traps to keep pests away from plants.
