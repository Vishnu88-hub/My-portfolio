Step 4: Organic Fertilization

Organic fertilization is essential for maintaining soil fertility and promoting healthy plant growth. It involves using natural fertilizers such as compost, vermicompost, manure, and biofertilizers.

Types of Organic Fertilizers:

  • Compost: Decomposed organic matter that enriches the soil.
  • Vermicompost: Nutrient-rich compost produced by earthworms.
  • Green Manure: Cover crops that are plowed into the soil to enhance fertility.
  • Biofertilizers: Microbial inoculants that improve nutrient availability.

Applying these organic fertilizers improves soil structure, water retention, and microbial activity, leading to sustainable agriculture.
