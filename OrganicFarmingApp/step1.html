Step-by-Step Guide to Organic Farming

Step 1: Soil Preparation

Ensure your soil is rich in organic matter by using compost and green manure.

Step 2: Choosing Organic Seeds

Select certified organic seeds that are free from genetic modifications and chemicals.

Step 3: Natural Pest Control

Use natural predators, neem oil, and crop rotation to protect crops from pests.

Step 4: Organic Fertilization

Apply compost, vermicompost, and organic liquid fertilizers for healthy crop growth.

Step 5: Harvesting and Certification

Follow proper harvesting techniques and apply for organic certification.
