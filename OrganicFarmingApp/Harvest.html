Step 5: Harvesting and Certification

Harvesting organic crops at the right time ensures the best quality produce. Follow these steps:

  • Monitor plant maturity and harvest at peak ripeness.
  • Use clean tools to avoid contamination.
  • Store harvested crops in a cool, dry place.
  • Apply for organic certification to ensure compliance with standards.

Organic certification involves thorough inspections, record-keeping, and adherence to sustainable farming practices.
