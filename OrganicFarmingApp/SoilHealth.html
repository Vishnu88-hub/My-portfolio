Soil Health

Maintaining good soil health is crucial for organic farming. It ensures plant growth, enhances microbial activity, and improves water retention. Here are some key factors for maintaining soil health:

  • Use organic compost and green manure.
  • Practice crop rotation to prevent nutrient depletion.
  • Avoid excessive tilling to preserve soil structure.
  • Maintain proper pH balance with natural amendments.
  • Encourage beneficial soil organisms for better fertility.
